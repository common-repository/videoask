= Video-driven UX =
VideoAsk makes collecting responses from your audience easy & engaging by leveraging the power of video.

* Create a VideoAsk in seconds on [videoask.com/app](https://videoask.com/app)
* Embed it on your website by pasting the link in the block settings
* Collect answers on any device. Video and audio responses are transcribed automatically

= Meet people in your 'Inbox' =
Manage your interactions with the VideoAsk inbox and get face to face with your audience.

* Reply to new leads via email or video (coming soon)
* Enable email/push notifications
* Export all you answers to .csv

== Installation ==
1. Add the plugin folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins’ menu in WordPress
3. Add your VideoAsk through the “Add block” button that has been added to post/pages editor aside of the Media Button
4. Add your VideoAsk URL and select from the options available

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
* How can I add a VideoAsk to my content?
 * Click the “Add block“ button, which opens a search field. Look for VideoAsk from your installed blocks. Once added, click on your new block and then click the Gear icon to open your VideoAsk block settings. Paste the URL of the VideoAsk you want to embed, then choose your embed options.
